Kawhi Leonard

Who is Kawhi Leonard?

The "Klaw" as many NBA fans like to call him, has grew up loving basketball...and it shows. Unfortunately, I didn't start following his career until he started playing with the Raptors from 2018 to 2019. However, now that he led the Raptors to victory, he holds a special place in my heart, and many other Canadians hearts as well.

History of Kawhi

Kawhi Leonard is 28 years old and was born on June 29, 1991 in California. His career started in 2009 at San Diego State University when he led his team to championships with an impressive 12 points and 10 rebound game. The Klaw started his paid career with San Antonio Spurs in 2011 and played with them till 2018. Later in the season, he was nominated as "Rookie of the Year". After tensions with the Spurs, Kawhi requested a trade with the Toronto Raptors in 2018. During his time with the Raptors, he led the team to their first NBA championship. With his help, the Raptors defeated the Golden State Warriors in a 4-2 win. Kawhi wanted to move back home, so he got traded to the Clippers in 2019.
